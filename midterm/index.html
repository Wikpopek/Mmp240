Euphoria

2019, HBO, 3 Seasons

Euphoria is a bold and visually stunning drama that follows a group of teenagers as they navigate love, identity, trauma, and addiction. The story centers on Rue (Zendaya), a troubled young woman struggling with substance abuse while searching for meaning in a chaotic world.

Creator:Sam Levinson

Cast:Zendaya, Hunter Schafer, Sydney Sweeney, Alexa Demie, Jacob Elordi, Maude Apatow, Angus Cloud, Barbie Ferreira, Dominic Fike, Storm Reid, Eric Dane, Nika King, Colman Domingo

Genres:Drama, Teen drama, Psychological drama
